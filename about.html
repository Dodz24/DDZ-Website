ORIGIN

DDZ was born out of a passion for elegance and self-expression. Our goal is to create pieces that speak beyond trends — timeless clothing that reflects individuality, creativity, and the culture of those who dare to

STAND OUT.

FOUNDER

Michael Francis Noel M. Oliveros, a BS in Computer Science Graduate, proposed the idea of creating the website to his peers, aiming to combine creativity with technology. Michael also took the lead in developing the website from scratch, ensuring its design and functionality matched the vision. With his skills and dedication, the foundation of the website “DDZ” was built and brought to life.
